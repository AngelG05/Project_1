Being a senior from the same college, let me first breakdown societies into first technical and non-technical :

1. Technical - Societies that actually help you persue your dream, help you code, make good connections and guide you through the coding journey most probably, these include - GDSC, CSI, NSUT.AI, Codechef and some others.
